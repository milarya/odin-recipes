Scrambled Eggs

Simple scrambled eggs

A savoury breakfast side dish. Double all amounts to make this a full dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 50 ml milk
  • 1 bunch of chives
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 dash of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Add milk, salt and pepper and whisk until not completely mixed
  3. Melt butter in a pan on medium heat
  4. Add egg mixture to pan
  5. Let egg solidity for a few seconds, then scramble into bite-sizes pieces
  6. Cook until egg is nearly done, then put onto plate
  7. Egg will finish with leftover heat on the plate
  8. Sprinkle chives on top
  9. Add more salt and pepper to taste
