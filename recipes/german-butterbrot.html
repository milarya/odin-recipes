Ingredients

  • 1 slice of German rye bread (from bakery)
  • 1 slice of cheese (preferably Emmentaler)
  • 10 g butter
  • some cucumber slices

Steps

  1. Spread butter on bread
  2. Add cheese to buttered bread
  3. Add some cucumber slices on top
  4. Enjoy!
