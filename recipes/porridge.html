Porridge

Yummy porridge with bananas

This yummy porridge is easy to make and tastes delicious. Perfect for breakfast or as a quick and tasty evening snack.

Ingredients

  • 50 g oats
  • 150 ml water
  • 1 medium banana
  • 50 ml cream
  • 1 packet vanilla sugar
  • 10 dashes of cinnamon
  • 1 dash of cardamom
  • 1 dash of cloves
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • optional: Nutella
  • optional: orange sugar

Steps

  1. Add oats and water to a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Add vanilla sugar, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves and salt
  3. Bring pot to a simmer
  4. Chop banana into bite-sized chunks (e.g. 1cmx1cm) and add to pot
  5. Stir until porridge has reached desired creaminess
  6. Add cream and bring to a boil
  7. Remove pot from stove and pour porridge into bowl
  8. Optional: Stir in some Nutella and/or sprinkle orange sugar on top
